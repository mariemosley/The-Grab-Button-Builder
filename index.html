The Grab Button Builder

Create an HTML grab button for your blog's sidebar, without writing any code!

This button builder will give you the same modern HTML grab button you'd get if you used my tutorial on Code it Pretty. The button works on Blogger, Wordpress.com, Wordpress.org, and Typepad blogs.

Before you get started, upload your button image to your favorite image host and copy the image's direct URL. Not sure how to get your image URL? Open the image host tutorial for detailed instructions.

Click on your image host to see where you can find your image URL.

  • Photobucket

    • Under "Links to share this photo", click the "Direct" link. The link will automatically copy itself to your clipboard.

    Paste that link into the "Image URL" field on this page.

    Close tutorial

  • Picasa

    • Click on your image in your Picasa album to get to the full-size image page. Then, click the "Link to this Photo" link.

    If the "Select Size" dropdown menu is showing, set it to "Original Size" (if the size menu isn't showing, don't worry). Then, click the checkbox next to "Image only (no link)". Copy the link in the "Embed Image" field and paste it into the "Image URL" field on this page.

    Close tutorial

  • Wordpress

    • After you upload your image to your media library, click the "Edit" link beside its thumbnail. On the "Edit Media" page, copy the URL in the "File URL" field.

    Paste that link into the "Image URL" field on this page.

    Close tutorial

  • Other

    • Is your favorite image host missing from the list? No problem! You can still get a direct URL for your image.

    Find the page in your album that shows you a full-sized version of your image. Check for link options or embed options. The direct URL will be in link format without any HTML or BBCode surrounding it. It should look something like this:

    http://www.example.com/username/image-name.png

    If you can't find a direct link option, check your host's terms of service and make sure you are allowed to directly link to images. Flickr and some other image hosts don't allow direct links. If your host doesn't allow direct links, try Photobucket or Picasa.

    Close tutorial
